Mentoring

ETE’s mentoring programme aims to link schools with qualified coaches and leaders who inspire learners to strive to achieve their highest potential. This is done through career talks in schools, organizing opportunities for job shadowing and supporting teacher seminars so that they can be better equipped to mentor pupils. The team aspires to make known to learners about opportunities available to them locally and globally. The team is constantly searching for opportunities that can be of benefit to the school learner, broaden their minds and foster in them a global perspective.

Leadreship Training

ETE partners with organisations that offer leadership training and s upports pupils who display leadership talent and aptitude to specialized leadership courses. In line with the principle of equity, the team believes that every child has the potential to be a leader starting by having a leader mentality by taking charge of their own learning and life and provides basic leadership training to all pupils.

Holistic Education

ETE recognizes that an all rounded education is critical for successful living. Academic results are not the primary focus of ETE but rather that each child has an equitable opportunity to learn what they require to lead a successful life in their own terms and for the benefit of the community as a whole. In this regard, ETE supports extra-curricular activities including dance, spoken word, home science, art and craft, music and sport, hairdressing, carpentry and masonry. The team encourages participation in 4K Clubs, Scouts, Girl Guide, St. John’s movements in schools to nurture team work, creativity and a culture of service in learners.
